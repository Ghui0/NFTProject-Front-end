Ray I

The Downfall of Neptune Empire


     Everything noble has a beginning and an end, as impossible as something may appear as easily it can diminish and disappear right in front of your eyes. And that is exactly what happened right before my eyes, no one would have thought, that our planet, once having conquered the whole solar system, would end up in a hopeless situation like this. Not a single citizen of the planet Neptune was scared during the leadership of our emperor Neptuss, who managed to conquer all nine planets of the solar system with his five chosen, most powerful generals, who were called the five rising stars. Neptuss was irreplaceable, he was like a miracle in this world, I have never seen a person who had nearly the same knowledge and capabilities that the emperor had. Therefore, everyone knew that nothing would endanger the planet before Neptuss was alive and sane. Huh… How silly was I, of course Neptuss was a noble person, but he could not defeat seven planets all by himself, additionally when traitors were eating our planet inside out. It was all my fault, it was my duty to eradicate inner revolution, even before the beginning, it was my mission, I was one of the five rising stars. Neptuss was really intelligent, he realized what was happening from the beginning and this noble person put faith into me, one of his best generals, but I made him frustrate. Because of my impatience I was trying to do everything quickly, in order to join the war with the other planets and because of me being impatient I could not sense the large amount of jealousy my best friend Grid was carrying. I cannot forgive myself for dooming the entire population of our planet to death. I clearly remember that day, the beginning of the end, the day when the great war started. Neptuss was self-confident and powerful as always. Therefore, people believed in him and I was not an exception.


     In a huge darkened hall, which had a ceiling so tall, that nobody was able to see the end was standing Neptuss, there were five rising stars behind him, they represented the power of Neptuss. An enormous gate opens, such a huge amount of weight made everything tremble. As soon as the gate opened light came into the hall, Neptuss went outside and saw the whole population of Neptune right in front of his nose spread out, they were welcoming our emperor. In a matter of seconds everybody went silent in an honor of the emperor and he started his speech:


“My Neptunians, I am aware that you are terrified. The seven planets declared a war against us, I reckon that they have forgotten what actually is the power of Neptune. There is nothing to be afraid of, our planet is undefeatable, nobody is able to kneel us until we are together. Today I demand my nation to give me power, with your support I – Neptuss promise that our planet will reclaim the lost glory!”


     Everyone started exulting, everyone was clapping for the emperor. Someone may be astonished that people were happy before starting a war, but it was mainly because everybody believed in the abilities of Neptuss, they were entirely sure that their leader would be victorious in every single war. Everyone was happy, but one man, my best friend, the emperor′s advisor, Grid. He was full of envy, he longed for the power. I noticed that, but could not believe, I did not want to believe that my best friend would betray us.


     The war Began! Enemies were invading us non-stop, their army had no end. The sky was full of aircrafts, but these huge armies disappeared in a matter of minutes. every time they tried to conquer our planet, they were defeated easily. Our forces were quickly moving forward and occupying more and more territories. Out of the seven planets only one was left. The last planet was Mars. That appeared to be the most difficult planet to defeat. Battles lasted days and during those days thousands of Neptunian soldiers and tens of thousands of Martians. That was the moment when I lost my patience, I just could not believe that someone would dare and rebel in out planet, despite the fact that the emperor did not deny that possibility. I could not keep watching that disastrous war from behind the scenes. I sat in my aircraft and went straight toward the battlefield on full speed. That day the whole planet was soaked with blood. I could easily take thousands of lives. in that battle I was given a name of the demon of Neptune. The war which had already lasted a month, ended in just a few hours. That made me nervous. Yes, I was the strongest out of the five rising stars, but the difference between the two sides of the battle should not have been that huge with only my helping hand. Every soldier was delighted. One more rebellious planet was defeated, or was it actually? Suddenly, there was a message of help from our planet. All of the five generals headed straight to our planet and forgot about the Mars, occupied territories and the possibility that enemies could take the control back over their planet. At that time our only worry was to find out what happened on Neptune, that soldiers needed help. Our only hope was the protective layer which was all around the Neptune, it was indestructible and the only way to open it was from inside. While approaching the planet there was an explosion which shut down our aircrafts. One of the generals said:


“It is impossible, the barrier is blocked, we cannot get in, how could our enemies cross the barrier.”


     Nobody had an answer to that question. everyone′s breath stopped. We were standing outside the barrier watching our planet getting destroyed unable to do anything. Our emperor was fighting until the last breath to defend his planet. As it seemed, the traitor, advisor of Neptuss and my best friend Grid whom I had known all my life shut down the barrier which was created by Bor one of the five rising stars. The Titanium dome was supposed to be a perfect and impenetrable barrier. That made all of us worry, especially me. I had been through a lot of wars, I had been wounded many times, but I had never felt more pain in my entire life, than that. No weapon cuts deeper than your best friend′s betrayal. I was furious, I could not control myself. I used all my weapons to cut through the barrier, but I was unable to even scratch it, even with the weapons that killed thousand of enemies and aircrafts in seconds. I could not do anything against the Titanium barrier, now I knew why it was called the Impenetrable Layer of Neptune. We could just watch the battle scenes of the emperor from the outside. The battle was uneven. enemy armies had no end. They were falling on each other lifeless. Nobody could touch Neptuss. At the moment of the triumph, when victory was close, the traitor cut straight into the emperor′s back with his spear. Others started attacking Neptuss while he was kneeling, but he was not giving up, he as fighting until he could breath. It was unbelievable even I, the strongest out of the five rising starts, could never defeat the emperor. Of course, I knew that he was far more powerful, but that was a whole different level. He was moving so swiftly, that we could not even see hm, even when he was injured. In a couple of minutes not a single soldier was standing. There was only one traitor and Neptuss approached him slowly. Grid could not even move because of fear. Neptuss finally approached him, looked at him in the eyes and started talking in frustrated voice:


“Get away from me, you traitor, you do not deserve to be one of our planet′s habitant. Even though you are a traitor, I order you one last time to fly away from our planet wherever you want, but make sure I never catch a glimpse of you and be grateful, that I did not slice you like your comrades”


     Grid was standing there frightened, he sat inside his aircraft and vanished in the boundless galaxy. The barrier was opened. Finally, we could walk freely, but it was already too late. We were not able to do anything. Every single one of us felt the pity, but in reality it was only my fault. Then, there was a warning that our enemies were approaching us. That time the emperor of Mars himself was coming with his best warriors. Possibly we could defeat them, but a lot of Neptunians would have died. Frustrated generals were looking at Neptuss and they did not know what to do, did we actually lose?


     Neptuss ordered us to start the Phoenix Operation. That was the only moment, when we hesitated after hearing the command. That was the only moment, when nobody wanted to follow their orders, because the Phoenix Operation meant saving every Neptunian in the cost of our emperor′s life. Every single soul would get into one stone, the crystal of life, they would freeze for some period. That was the only way to escape. Tactical retreat before we would be able to regain the strength we need. I was ordered to preserve that crystal. I, the one who was guilty for all that was happening there. I would be called the only weakness of the legendary Neptuss and that name would be the with me for the rest of my life. All of that was happening because of me, I did not obey the rules, I decided to get to the battlefield and left the planet. I could not have disappointed my emperor once more, but it seemed like I could not even do a simple task at that point. I lost self-confidence, so I denied the command. I decided to get to the battlefield, devote my heart and waste our enemies′ time as much as I could while my allies would take care of the Phoenix Operation. While I was fighting with my enemies the operation had already ended and somehow I ended up picking up the crystal of life. Generals gave me some time to retreat. They sacrificed themselves and they made me fly away from the planet with the crystal. A tear ran down my face. I was the best warrior out of the five rising stars, I have been in many wars and stopped a lot of mighty warriors, but at that time I could not stop myself from crying. That was the moment when I lost everything: emperor, comrades, nation and a best friend. I just… wanted to die… I let go everything, aircraft lost its route, my eyes were closing slowly, but in the end, I realized that if I died, I could not fulfill my mission. Thousands of lives depended on me. It was my duty to survive and regain the lost glory for my nation. The war has not ended before I am breathing. Before this crystal exists, my nation is alive and so is the hope of victory.
